This is Us

We are a team of four Business Informatics students from the Free University of Bozen and we have been putting our hearts and souls into this project because we all have personally struggled with the social situation covid has caused us and we would love to make our mark and hopefully make a difference. If you want to learn more about us, visit our team page!

How did we come up with this

Being four young students, we have directly suffered the impact of this pandemic. We created this space for all to be able to connect and share thoughts, ideas and feelings with others, all anonimously, without stressing about the toxicity that we are brought to when using social networks. We built a platform in which anybody can develop ideas they are afraid to show, share them with the world and feel less alone in whatever thought they have running through their mind.
